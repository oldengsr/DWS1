Melted Snowman Cookies

These adorable Melted Snowman Cookies will put a smile on anyone's face this holiday!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Betty Crocker Sugar Cookie mix
  • 10 large marshmallows
  • Betty Crocker Cookie Icing (white and orange)
  • Wilton Decorating Icing Tubes in black, red, green, blue and pink

Directions

  1. Mix cookie mix according to the directions, then grab a golf ball sized chunk and squish it down until it's about 1/4 - 1/2 inch thick. Don't worry about the shape or how lumpy it is, the more misshapen the better!
  2. Bake according to Betty Crocker recipe directions.
  3. Once the cookies are cool, load them up with cookie icing and spread it around with the back of a spoon, let some dribble over the edge for a "puddle" look.
  4. Spray a microwave safe plate with cooking spray, and place marshmallows on the plate.
  5. Set the microwave for 30 seconds, but watch the marshmallows as they cook.
  6. Stop the microwave as soon as the marshmallows start to get puffy, do not let them double in size.
  7. Spray your fingers with cooking spray or grease them up with shortening, and carefully pull the marshmallows off, by the base, and set them on top of the frosted cookies.
  8. Then decorate them as you like with the Wilton frosting!
